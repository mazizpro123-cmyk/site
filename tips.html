☀️ Morning

Morning Routine

Start your day with a clean mouth and fresh breath

  1. 1
    Rinse with water

    Loosen overnight bacteria

  2. 2
    Brush for 2 minutes

    All 4 zones, 30 sec each

  3. 3
    Clean your tongue

    Back to front, 3-5 strokes

  4. 4
    Spit, don't rinse

    Let fluoride work longer

🌙 Night

Night Routine

The most important routine — don't skip it

  1. 1
    Floss all teeth

    Before brushing to loosen debris

  2. 2
    Brush for 2 minutes

    Gentle pressure, all surfaces

  3. 3
    Clean your tongue

    Remove bacteria from surface

  4. 4
    Mouthwash (optional)

    Fluoride rinse for extra protection

Expert Tips

Upgrade your oral
health today

💧

Drink More Water

Water washes away food particles and bacteria. It also keeps saliva production up — your mouth's natural defense against decay. Aim for 2 liters daily.

🥕

Eat Crunchy Veggies

Carrots, celery, and apples naturally scrub teeth and stimulate saliva flow.

🧀

Cheese & Dairy

High in calcium and casein — both strengthen tooth enamel naturally.

☕

Limit Coffee & Tea

These stain teeth. If you drink them, rinse with water immediately after.

🌿

Green Tea Helps

Contains catechins that kill bacteria responsible for gum disease and bad breath.

🦷

See Your Dentist Every 6 Months

Professional cleanings remove tartar that no brush can touch. Early cavity detection saves thousands in treatment costs later. Put it in your calendar now.

Regular checkups prevent 80% of dental emergencies
